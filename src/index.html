We envision a world where conversations are as impactful as they are engaging. That's why we are developing Sensemaker: an evolving toolkit, designed to help make sense of large-scale conversations.
Why Use Sensemaker Tools?

When conversations scale, nuance is often lost. Sensemaker preserves the richness of conversation while making it easier to identify patterns, surface areas of consensus and disagreement, and, ultimately, make informed decisions. From analyzing public input to understanding discourse, Sensemaker offers a smarter way to bring clarity to complexity, quickly.

We're continuing to explore new applications of Sensemaker and will be adding experimental features as we test them.

How It Works
See What Was Discussed

Identify key topics and themes from the conversation, providing a clear overview of what mattered most to participants.

Understand Points of Agreement & Debate

Reveal areas of consensus and divergence, helping users grasp common ground and differing perspectives at a glance.

Get a Conversation Digest

Break down each topic into an easy-to-read summary, making it simple to explore subtopics, key insights, and emerging themes.
