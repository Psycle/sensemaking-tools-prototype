The Opportunity

Bowling Green, Kentucky is on the brink of a major transformation. Over the next 25 years, Warren County—home to Bowling Green—is projected to nearly double in size. While much of this growth is driven by external forces, like the rapid expansion of Nashville just an hour south, the community has a choice: Will growth happen to us, or for us? The opportunity is now for the community to share its future development.

The Deliberation

In April 2025, Innovation Engine, in collaboration with The Computational Democracy Project and Jigsaw, with support from Google.org, launched What Could BG Be?—a month-long, community-wide online conversation designed to let the people of Warren County define their future.

The Outcome

Using Jigsaw’s Sensemaker tools, local leaders and community members gained a clearer, data-driven understanding of the conversation—uncovering actionable insights to guide Bowling Green’s growth.
