Transforming How We Understand
Conversation At-Scale

Sensemaker empowers users to see patterns, perspectives, and the shared meaning of any discussion – turning conversation into clear, actionable insights.

We envision a world where conversations are as impactful as they are engaging. That's why we are developing Sensemaker: an evolving toolkit, designed to help make sense of large-scale conversations.

Why Use
Sensemaker Tools?

When conversations scale, nuance is often lost. Sensemaker preserves the richness of conversation while making it easier to identify patterns, surface areas of consensus and disagreement, and, ultimately, make informed decisions. From analyzing public input to understanding discourse, Sensemaker offers a smarter way to bring clarity to complexity, quickly.

We're continuing to explore new applications of Sensemaker and will be adding experimental features as we test them.

How It Works

See What Was Discussed

Identify key topics and themes from the conversation, providing a clear overview of what mattered most to participants.

Understand Points of Agreement & Debate

Reveal areas of consensus and divergence, helping users grasp common ground and differing perspectives at a glance.

Get a Conversation Digest

Break down each topic into an easy-to-read summary, making it simple to explore subtopics, key insights, and emerging themes.

Who Sensemaker Tools Can Help

  • Deliberative Technology Platforms

    Tap into community insights and maintain meaningful engagement.

  • Community Leaders & Managers

    Quickly understand the nuance of large-scale conversations and surface shared priorities.

  • Decision Makers

    Understand and act on conversations with confidence.

  • Participants

    See how your voice contributes to the bigger picture and drives meaningful outcomes.

Pilot Program

What Could [Bowling Green] Be?

Enabling a Large-Scale Conversation in Kentucky

Featured Press

The Opportunity

Bowling Green, Kentucky is on the brink of a major transformation. Over the next 25 years, Warren County—home to Bowling Green—is projected to nearly double in size. While much of this growth is driven by external forces, like the rapid expansion of Nashville just an hour south, the community has a choice: Will growth happen to us, or for us? The opportunity is now for the community to share its future development.

Learn More

“...Growth can either happen to us, or for us.”

Warren County, Judge Executive Doug Gorman

The Deliberation

In April 2025, Innovation Engine, in collaboration with The Computational Democracy Project and Jigsaw, with support from Google.org, launched What Could BG Be?—a month-long, community-wide online conversation designed to let the people of Warren County define their future.

Read a Recap

The Outcome

Using Jigsaw’s Sensemaker tools, local leaders and community members gained a clearer, data-driven understanding of the conversation—uncovering actionable insights to guide Bowling Green’s growth.
